## Not Sure Where to Start?

Browse 80+ ready-made Lemonades in the Marketplace! Find one close to what you need, click 'Use Template,' and customize it to fit your business.

Templates are your shortcut - someone else already figured out the instructions, structure, and settings. You just add your unique knowledge and make it yours!

## Accessing the Marketplace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Marketplace">
    Click **Marketplace** in the top navigation bar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Templates">
    Explore categories and featured Lemonades
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Pre-Built Solutions

Each template includes:
- **Ready-to-use Instructions** – Optimized prompts that already work
- **Sample Knowledge** – Example files and context to show you how
- **Best Practice Settings** – Recommended configurations from experienced builders

## Using a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Templates">
    Find a template that matches your use case
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview">
    Click on a template to see details and try a demo
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Template">
    Click **"Use This Template"** - this creates your own copy to edit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make it Yours">
    The template is just a starting point - now add your own knowledge, tweak the instructions, and make it uniquely yours!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Templates are starting points, not final products! Always customize the Instructions and add your own Knowledge. The magic happens when you make it specific to YOUR business.
</Tip>
