### Welcome Message

The first message sent when someone starts a chat.

**Default:** "Hello {first_name}, how can I help you today?"

<Note>
Use `{first_name}` as a placeholder to automatically insert the user's first name!
</Note>

**Tips for good welcome messages:**
- Be friendly and inviting
- Briefly explain what the Lemonade can help with
- Ask an open-ended question to start the conversation

**Examples:**
```text
"Hi {first_name}! I'm your personal marketing assistant. What can I help you create today?"

"Welcome! I'm here to answer your questions about our products. What would you like to know?"

"Hey {first_name}! Ready to brainstorm some ideas? Let's get started!"
```

### Instructions

This is where you give your AI its personality and job description. Think of it like hiring someone - you're writing their role and guidelines.

<Note>
Developers call this a "system prompt" - now you know the lingo!
</Note>

**Default:** "You are a helpful chatbot"

**Tips for good instructions:**
- Be specific about the Lemonade's role
- Define its personality and tone
- Set boundaries on topics
- Include any specific knowledge or context

**Example:**
```text
You are a customer support assistant for TechCorp, a software company.

Your responsibilities:
- Answer questions about our products
- Help troubleshoot common issues
- Guide users to relevant documentation
- Escalate complex issues to human support

Tone: Professional but friendly. Use simple language.

Important: Never share pricing information. Direct pricing questions to sales@techcorp.com
```

Enable powerful additional features to make your Lemonade even better.

| Feature | Description | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Enable Search** | Let your Lemonade search the internet for current information | Off |
| **Enable File Upload** | Users can upload files during chat for analysis | On |
| **Enable Image Upload** | Users can share images for your Lemonade to understand | On |
| **Remember All Conversations** | Lemonade remembers all chats (persistent memory) | Off |
| **Developer Mode** | Connect via API or Zapier for automation | Off |

### Enable Search
Turn this on if your Lemonade needs access to current information beyond its training data.

### Enable File Upload
Allows users to upload documents during a chat. Great for:
- Document analysis
- Data extraction
- File-based Q&A

### Enable Image Upload
Allows users to share images. Your Lemonade can:
- Describe images
- Analyze visual content
- Answer questions about images

### Remember All Conversations
When enabled, your Lemonade will remember all previous conversations.

<Warning>
This does NOT change your instructions - it only allows the Lemonade to recall what was discussed in previous chats.
</Warning>

### Developer Mode

If you're a developer (or work with one), this lets you connect your Lemonade to other apps and build custom integrations.

Not a developer? You can safely skip this!

### Deploy as an Employee via Email

Email your Lemonade directly from your inbox - like having an AI assistant you can email anytime.

**Setup:**
1. Add a custom email address (e.g., `support@ai.launchlemonade.app`)
2. Click **COPY EMAIL**, **SAVE TO GMAIL**, or **SAVE TO OUTLOOK**
3. Email from your registered address to start chatting

<Note>
For security, only emails from your registered email address will be processed.
</Note>

### Deploy as a ChatBot on your website

Embed your Lemonade as a chat widget on any website.

**Pricing:** [Contact us](https://launchlemonade.app/pricing/)

**Steps:**
1. Click **"CREATE EMBED MIX"**
2. Customize your branding
3. Get your embed code
4. Add to your website

### Deploy on your Domain to sell

Monetize your Lemonade with subscriptions - turn your AI into a business.

**Pricing:** [Contact us](https://launchlemonade.app/pricing/)
